Human Speed Detector

A device that measures how fast a person is moving.

  • 5,120 Etiam
  • 8,192 Magna
  • 2,048 Tempus
  • 4,096 Aliquam
  • 1,024 Nullam

At The Royal Academy, fitness tests are given a lot of importance as they help monitor a student's personal health and progress. One particular event in the fitness test is the 100m dash. The results for this are often not accurate due to manual timing methods. To solve this problem, the human speed detector can be used to precisely measure running speed.
