Introduction

The following are my project ideas! All of them focus on tackling problems that we face in school but can also go beyond that.

SmartFollow Backpack

  • What it is

    A SmartFollow backpack is a backpack that follows you by itself using sensors and motors.

  • How it works

    It uses sensors to detect where you are and motors to move after you.

  • Who can use it

    For people who want to reduce the effort of carrying heavy bags.
