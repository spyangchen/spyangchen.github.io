Meteor Detector

To help find and study meteors

Inspiration

Watching meteors at night is one of the best feelings in the world for me. This is what inspired me to come up with the idea of a meteor detector. This detector will be able to detect meteors entering the atmosphere by sensing light flashes or through sounds.

Basic Components

  • Microphone
  • Light sensor
  • Circuit board
  • LCD display
  • Battery or USB power
  • Mount for best results

How It Works

Uses sensors to detect bright flashes (light sensor) or sound (microphone).
Processes signals.
Outputs data visually for analysis.

Who Will Use It?

  • Students and educators: For learning and science projects.
  • Researchers: For people collecting data on meteor activity.
  • Anyone curious about space: For people wanting to track and enjoy meteor events.
